## Content Strategy Rules

### What's Working in SEO Right Now
1. **Deep, actionable guides** (2,000-3,500 words)
2. **Data-driven analysis** with specific numbers
3. **Step-by-step frameworks** readers can implement immediately
4. **Embedded YouTube videos** (boost rankings)
5. **Clean structure**: H2/H3 hierarchy, bullet lists, scannable
6. **Personal narratives** mixed with technical advice
7. **Comparison content** (Before/After, Old vs New)

### Anti-AI Writing Enforcement
- ❌ **NO em dashes (—)** → Use parentheses or periods
- ❌ **NO AI filler words**: delve, tapestry, navigating, demystify, crucial, landscape
- ❌ **NO repetitive structures**: "Introductory clause, main clause" every sentence
- ✅ **Varied sentence length**: Mix short punchy statements with longer explanations
- ✅ **Human voice**: Use contractions, address reader directly, show personality
- ✅ **Active voice** > Passive voice
