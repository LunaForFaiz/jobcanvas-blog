# JobCanvas.ai Blog

Autonomous SEO-optimized blog content for JobCanvas.ai, focused on resume optimization, career strategies, and job market analysis.

## Structure

```
jobcanvas-blog/
├── blog_orchestrator.md          # Daily strategy and orchestration log
├── writers/                       # Writer persona profiles
│   ├── marcus_profile.md         # Technical Recruiter persona
│   ├── elena_profile.md          # Career Strategist persona
│   └── julian_profile.md         # Industry Analyst persona
├── posts/                         # Published blog posts (markdown)
│   └── YYYY-MM-DD-post-slug.md
├── images/                        # Blog images and graphics
└── IMAGE_SOURCES.md               # Image sourcing guide

## Writer Personas

### Marcus (Technical Recruiter)
- **Focus:** ATS optimization, AI in hiring, technical resume structures
- **Tone:** Direct, authoritative, actionable
- **Word Count:** 2,500-3,000

### Elena (Career Strategist)
- **Focus:** Interviewing, soft skills, career transitions
- **Tone:** Empathetic, narrative-driven, psychological
- **Word Count:** 2,200-2,800

### Julian (Industry Analyst)
- **Focus:** Job market trends, hiring data, future of work
- **Tone:** Analytical, data-heavy, objective
- **Word Count:** 2,400-3,000

## Anti-AI Writing Rules (Enforced)

- ❌ NO em dashes (—)
- ❌ NO AI filler: delve, tapestry, navigating, demystify, crucial, landscape
- ❌ NO repetitive sentence structures
- ✅ Varied sentence length
- ✅ Human voice with contractions
- ✅ Active voice preferred

## SEO Strategy

Target high-intent keywords in career, resume optimization, and AI technology spaces:

- ATS optimization 2026
- AI resume trends
- skills-first resume
- STAR interview method
- job market trends 2026
- career transition strategies

## Deployment

Posts are written in markdown, committed to this repository, and deployed via Vercel integration.

## Daily Workflow

1. **Research:** Daily SEO trends and keyword analysis
2. **Strategy:** Update `blog_orchestrator.md` with findings
3. **Assignment:** Assign topics to three writer personas
4. **Drafting:** Generate 3 SEO-optimized posts (markdown)
5. **Images:** Source hero + interstitial images
6. **Git:** Commit and push to repository
7. **Verify:** Check Vercel deployment and live URLs
